What's Next in My Journey

📝 Entrance Exam Preparation

I will begin preparing for the entrance exams that will determine my eligibility for further studies in medicine.

🎓 Obtain a Bachelor's Degree

After passing the entrance exams, I will obtain a bachelor's degree, which is a crucial step toward applying to medical school.

🧪 Participate in Internships

During my studies, I will participate in internships to gain hands-on experience in the medical field.

🏫 Apply to an Accredited School

After completing my bachelor's degree, I will apply to an accredited medical school that aligns with my goals and values.

📚 Complete Coursework

At medical school, I will complete rigorous coursework to deepen my understanding of medical concepts and patient care.

✅ Pass Licensing Exams

I will prepare for and pass licensing exams to become a certified healthcare professional.

🎯 Post-Graduate Studies

If desired, I will pursue post-graduate studies to specialize in a particular area of healthcare or medicine.

🎉 Work in the Field

After completing my studies and certifications, I will start working in the medical field, contributing to healthcare and helping those in need.
